Pokebec Nigeria Limited

Pokebec Nigeria Limited is the premier name in the Oil and Gas, Vessel shipping and construction industry today, it is a brand synonymous with quality, reliability and timely delivery. This reputation has been earned through over many years of experience of Importation and Exportaion of Oil and Gas products, Cargo or vessel shipping and construction of Roads, Highways, Buildings, Drainage system, and other forms of infrastructure all around Nigeria. We have earned wealth of technical and physical capacity and an experienced team of highly trained expatriates & non-expatriates capable of delivering world class projects anywhere & any time.

Our Vision

  • To become a trusted construction company to our esteemed clients and in the Nigerian business sector.
  • To positively affect the Oil and Gas and construction industry through our innovative and quality services deliverance.

Our Objectives

  • Our work quality and performance should comply with the required measures and criteria.
  • Protect safety and health of our employees as well as third parties.
  • Protect work and surrounding environment, prevent pollution and save resources.
  • Achieving suitable profit to ensure sustainability.
  • Full customer satisfaction as per specification.

Our Mission

  • Our mission here at Pokebec Nigeria Limited is to utilize first class technologies in the design and execution of civil and infrastructural projects
  • We are committed to provide high standard services and products that are fit for the purpose.
  • We aim at satisfying our customers by providing best value for money.
  • We are committed to quality and preserving the environment.
  • We believe that our development is strongly related to the development of our human resources through training.

Our Value

  • Our core value here at Pokebec Nigeria Limited is our quality service delivery through our highly skilled and professional staff.
  • We uphold our reputation for integrity in the marketplace and provide an ethical work environment for all our employees.
  • Quality Oil and Gas and Vessel Shipping construction services to our various clients in Nigeria.

Pokebec Nigeria Limited is a leading Oil and Gas and construction company in Nigeria with over many years working experience in delivering value-added civil and infrastructural projects in Nigeria.
